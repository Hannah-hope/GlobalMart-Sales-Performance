# 📊 GlobalMart Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## 🧠 Project Overview
This project presents an end-to-end sales and profitability analysis of GlobalMart, a multinational retail company operating across Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology categories. 

The dashboard evaluates $1.08M in sales and $326.21K in profit (30.23% margin), uncovering key performance drivers, operational inefficiencies, and profit leakage through a structured, data-driven approach.
---

## 🎯 Business Problem
This analysis addresses key business questions:
1. Which regions and states drive the most revenue?
2. Which product categories and sub-categories contribute most to profit?
3. What is the impact of discounting on profitability?
4. How efficient are operations, particularly shipping performance?

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales and profit performance  
- Identify high-performing and underperforming regions  
- Detect profit leakage at sub-category level  
- Assess relationship between discounts and profit margins  
- Provide actionable recommendations for growth

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Overall Performance
- Total Sales: $1.08M  
- Total Profit: $326.21K  
- Profit Margin: 30.23%  
- Strong profitability, but **unstable year-on-year performance**  

Performance peaked in 2023 ($834K sales) but declined significantly in 2024, indicating reliance on specific high-performing periods rather than consistent growth. 

---

### 🌍 Regional Performance
- West region contributes ~67% of total sales (dominant driver)  
- Top states: California, New York, Texas  
- Central region shows highest negative profit  

High late shipment rates (40–50%) across all regions suggest operational inefficiencies impacting profitability and customer experience.

---

### 📦 Product Performance
- Office Supplies leads with ~$0.6M in sales  
- High-margin sub-categories:
  - Binders (~71%)
  - Chairs (~70%)
  - Pens (~69%)

Low-performing sub-categories such as Paper (~0.6%) and Tables (~22%) indicate pricing inefficiencies and discount-driven losses.

---

### ⚠️ Profit Leakage
- Profit Leakage: ~-0.08% to -0.10%  
- 22 loss-making orders | 19 loss-making products  
- Avg Discount on loss-making transactions: ~30–31%

Furniture (Tables) is the primary loss driver, with leakage concentrated in specific products and regions rather than across the entire business.

---

### 🚚 Operational Efficiency
- Avg Shipping Time: ~4.5 days  
- Late Shipment Rate: ~41%  

Indicates consistent delays across regions, contributing to inefficiencies and potential customer dissatisfaction.

---

## 💡 Recommendations
1. Introduce discount caps (20–25%) to protect margins  
2. Implement margin-based pricing controls  
3. Scale high-margin sub-categories (Binders, Chairs, Pens)  
4. Reprice or discontinue low-margin products (Tables, Paper)  
5. Reduce dependency on the West region  
6. Improve logistics to reduce late shipment rates  
7. Create alerts for loss-making orders/products  
8. Bundle low-margin products with high-margin ones  
9. Enable automated data refresh for real-time insights
